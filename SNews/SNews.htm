    Electric Customers Forced to Buy Smart Meters
    A smart meter is an electronic device that records consumption of electric energy and is capable of sending the information it captures. The Maryland Public Service Commission issued an order late February (2014) allowing electric companies to charge household and small commercial customers a one-time $75 fee, plus monthly fees, for opting not to have one of the advanced smart meters.
